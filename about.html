Who We Are

Elimuhub Education Consultants is a Nairobi-based tutoring and homeschooling center dedicated to personalized learning solutions. We believe that every learner is unique, and we provide flexible, affordable, and effective educational support to ensure success at every stage of learning.

With a team of experienced tutors across international and Kenyan curricula, we have supported students from diverse backgrounds, helping them excel in IGCSE, IB, American K-12, British Curriculum, CBC, KCPE, KCSE, and adult education.

Our Vision & Mission

Our mission is to empower learners of all ages to achieve their academic and personal goals through innovative, one-on-one, and group learning support. We envision a future where every learner in Kenya has access to quality, flexible, and affordable education tailored to their needs.
