What We Offer

At Elimuhub Education Consultants, we provide personalized tutoring, homeschooling, and academic support services designed to fit each learner’s unique needs. Whether you’re a parent seeking home-based lessons, a student preparing for exams, or an adult returning to education, we’ve got you covered.

🏫 One-on-One Tutoring

Private, individualized tutoring across multiple subjects and levels. Our expert tutors help learners grasp concepts faster and build academic confidence.

📖 Homeschooling Programs

Customized homeschooling support for learners of all ages, aligned with IGCSE, IB, American, British, CBC, and Kenyan 8-4-4 curriculums.

👩‍🏫 After-School Support

Flexible after-school tutoring sessions designed to reinforce classroom learning, improve grades, and strengthen study habits.

📚 Exam Preparation

Targeted coaching for KCPE, KCSE, IGCSE, and IB exams. We focus on exam techniques, revision strategies, and confidence-building.

🎓 Adult Education

We support adult learners with literacy, career development, and flexible study schedules tailored to personal and professional needs.

🌍 International Curriculums

Tutoring for international syllabuses including IGCSE, IB, and American K–12. Perfect for families seeking global-standard education.
